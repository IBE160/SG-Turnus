On "Cognitive Load"

Your next step: Define "Intrinsic" and "Extraneous" load in your own words.

Cognitive load refers to the mental effort used in your working memory. For effective learning, it's crucial to manage this by focusing on the task's inherent difficulty (intrinsic load) and reducing distractions (extraneous load).
