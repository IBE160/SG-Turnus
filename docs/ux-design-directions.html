What is Cognitive Load?

Action: Define Intrinsic & Extraneous Load.

Cognitive load refers to the total amount of mental effort being used in the working memory. It is critical to manage this for effective learning.

  • Intrinsic Load: Inherent difficulty of the task.
  • Extraneous Load: Caused by poor design or presentation.
